What is Robovian?

Robovian is an AI and Robotics learning app designed to make technical learning simple, practical, and student-friendly. It combines guided learning, project discovery, coding help, circuit understanding, hardware guidance, and intelligent support through RoboGenie, the built-in AI assistant.

Robovian is not just an app for reading concepts. It is a platform where students can learn, ask questions, discover projects, solve doubts, and grow step by step from beginner level to advanced robotics understanding.
